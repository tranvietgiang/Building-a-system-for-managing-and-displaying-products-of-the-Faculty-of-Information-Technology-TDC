HUONG DAN SU DUNG WEBSITE THEO VAI TRO

Website dung de trung bay, quan ly va duyet san pham/do an sinh vien cua khoa Cong nghe thong tin TDC.
He thong co 4 vai tro chinh: Visitor, Student, Teacher va Admin.


1. VISITOR - Khach tham quan

Visitor la nguoi chua dang nhap he thong.

Chuc nang chinh:
- Xem trang trung bay san pham sinh vien.
- Xem danh sach san pham da duoc cong khai/da duyet.
- Loc san pham theo chuyen nganh.
- Sap xep san pham theo moi nhat, xem nhieu nhat hoac yeu thich nhat.
- Tim kiem san pham bang tim kiem thuong hoac AI Search.
- Xem chi tiet san pham.
- Tang luot xem khi mo chi tiet san pham.
- Tha yeu thich/like san pham.
- Su dung ChatBox AI de hoi dap thong tin co ban.
- Chuyen den trang dang nhap khi can dung tai khoan.

Cach su dung:
1. Truy cap trang /nckh-visitor.
2. Dung o tim kiem de nhap ten san pham, cong nghe, chuyen nganh hoac tu khoa lien quan.
3. Chon chuyen nganh neu muon loc ket qua.
4. Chon kieu sap xep: moi nhat, xem nhieu nhat hoac yeu thich nhat.
5. Bat AI neu muon tim kiem thong minh theo ngu canh.
6. Bam "Xem chi tiet" de xem thong tin day du cua san pham.


2. STUDENT - Sinh vien

Student la nguoi dung co tai khoan sinh vien, dung de quan ly san pham/do an cua minh.

Chuc nang chinh:
- Dang nhap vao he thong.
- Xem trang chu sinh vien.
- Xem thong tin ca nhan, chuyen nganh va thong ke san pham.
- Xem danh sach san pham cua ban than.
- Loc san pham theo trang thai: tat ca, cho duyet, da duyet, bi tu choi.
- Tim kiem san pham bang tim kiem thuong hoac AI.
- Xem chi tiet san pham cua minh.
- Dang tai san pham moi.
- Chinh sua san pham da dang.
- Xoa san pham cua minh.
- Xem trang thai duyet cua san pham.
- Su dung ChatBox AI de hoi dap hoac ho tro tim kiem y tuong/thong tin.
- Cap nhat ho so ca nhan va doi mat khau.

Cach dang san pham:
1. Dang nhap bang tai khoan sinh vien.
2. Vao trang /nckh-student.
3. Bam nut dang san pham hoac truy cap /upload.
4. Nhap thong tin co ban cua san pham: tieu de, mo ta, chuyen nganh, danh muc.
5. Nhap thong tin rieng theo chuyen nganh neu co, vi du: CNTT, AI, Mang may tinh, Thiet ke do hoa.
6. Them anh dai dien, hinh anh minh hoa, file, link GitHub hoac link demo neu co.
7. Gui san pham len he thong.
8. San pham se o trang thai "cho duyet" cho den khi giao vien/admin xu ly.

Cach chinh sua san pham:
1. Vao danh sach san pham cua minh.
2. Chon san pham can sua.
3. Bam sua/chinh sua.
4. Cap nhat noi dung can thay doi.
5. Luu lai va cho duyet neu he thong yeu cau.

Cach xoa san pham:
1. Vao danh sach san pham cua minh.
2. Chon nut xoa tren san pham can xoa.
3. Xac nhan xoa.


3. TEACHER - Giang vien

Teacher la nguoi dung co quyen xem va duyet san pham cua sinh vien theo pham vi duoc phan cong.

Chuc nang chinh:
- Dang nhap vao he thong.
- Xem dashboard giang vien.
- Xem thong tin ca nhan, email va chuyen nganh phu trach.
- Xem thong ke san pham: tong san pham, cho duyet, da duyet, bi tu choi.
- Xem danh sach san pham cho duyet.
- Xem danh sach san pham da duyet.
- Xem danh sach san pham bi tu choi.
- Loc san pham theo trang thai.
- Xem chi tiet san pham sinh vien.
- Mo anh san pham de xem lon.
- Kiem tra link GitHub va link demo neu san pham co cung cap.
- Duyet san pham.
- Tu choi san pham va gui ly do/nhan xet.
- Tim kiem san pham bang AI Search.
- So sanh san pham bang chuc nang AI Compare.
- Su dung ChatBox AI de ho tro tim kiem va danh gia thong tin.
- Cap nhat ho so ca nhan va doi mat khau.

Cach duyet san pham:
1. Dang nhap bang tai khoan giang vien.
2. Vao trang /nckh-teacher.
3. Chon tab "Cho duyet".
4. Bam "Xem chi tiet" tren san pham can kiem tra.
5. Doc thong tin, mo hinh anh, file, link GitHub hoac demo neu co.
6. Neu san pham hop le, bam duyet.
7. Neu san pham chua dat, bam tu choi va nhap ly do de sinh vien biet can sua gi.

Cach xem san pham theo trang thai:
1. Vao trang giang vien.
2. Bam vao thong ke hoac tab: Cho duyet, Da duyet, Tu choi.
3. He thong hien thi danh sach san pham tuong ung.


4. ADMIN - Quan tri vien

Admin la nguoi co quyen quan tri toan bo he thong.

Chuc nang chinh:
- Dang nhap vao trang quan tri.
- Xem Dashboard tong quan.
- Xem thong ke tong nguoi dung, tong san pham, san pham cho duyet, so chuyen nganh.
- Xem bieu do san pham theo chuyen nganh.
- Xem bieu do san pham theo thang.
- Xem tinh trang san pham: da duyet, cho duyet, tu choi.
- Xem danh sach san pham moi nhat.
- Xem phan tich/gợi y tu AI neu he thong co du lieu.
- Quan ly nguoi dung.
- Quan ly san pham.
- Quan ly chuyen nganh.
- Xu ly yeu cau ho tro/quen mat khau trong muc Support.
- Xem danh muc.
- Truy cap phan cai dat he thong.
- Dang xuat khoi he thong.

4.1. Quan ly nguoi dung

Chuc nang:
- Xem danh sach nguoi dung.
- Tim kiem theo ma nguoi dung, ten hoac email.
- Loc nguoi dung theo vai tro: student, teacher, admin.
- Loc nguoi dung theo chuyen nganh.
- Them nguoi dung moi.
- Sua thong tin nguoi dung.
- Doi vai tro nguoi dung.
- Gan chuyen nganh va lop cho nguoi dung.
- Xoa nguoi dung.

Cach them nguoi dung:
1. Vao /nckh-admin.
2. Chon menu "Nguoi dung".
3. Nhap ma nguoi dung, ho ten, email, mat khau.
4. Chon vai tro: student, teacher hoac admin.
5. Chon chuyen nganh va lop neu can.
6. Bam "Them".

Cach sua nguoi dung:
1. Trong danh sach nguoi dung, bam nut sua.
2. Cap nhat thong tin can thay doi.
3. Neu khong doi mat khau thi de trong o mat khau.
4. Bam "Luu".

4.2. Quan ly san pham

Chuc nang:
- Xem danh sach tat ca san pham.
- Tim san pham theo ten.
- Loc san pham theo trang thai: cho duyet, da duyet, tu choi.
- Loc san pham theo chuyen nganh.
- Bat/tat AI Search de tim kiem thong minh.
- Xem so luot xem va luot thich cua san pham.
- Doi trang thai san pham.
- Xoa san pham.
- Chon so luong san pham hien thi moi trang.
- Chuyen trang trong danh sach san pham.

Cach doi trang thai san pham:
1. Vao menu "San pham".
2. Tim san pham can xu ly.
3. Tai cot trang thai, chon pending, approved hoac rejected.
4. He thong cap nhat trang thai san pham.

4.3. Quan ly chuyen nganh

Chuc nang:
- Xem danh sach chuyen nganh.
- Them chuyen nganh moi.
- Sua ten chuyen nganh, ma chuyen nganh va mo ta.
- Xoa chuyen nganh.
- Xem so luong san pham theo tung chuyen nganh.

Cach them chuyen nganh:
1. Vao menu "Chuyen nganh".
2. Nhap ten chuyen nganh.
3. Nhap ma chuyen nganh.
4. Nhap mo ta neu co.
5. Bam "Them".

Cach sua/xoa chuyen nganh:
1. Vao danh sach chuyen nganh.
2. Bam sua de cap nhat thong tin.
3. Bam xoa neu muon xoa chuyen nganh.
4. Xac nhan thao tac khi he thong hoi.

4.4. Support - Xu ly yeu cau quen mat khau

Chuc nang:
- Xem danh sach yeu cau khoi phuc mat khau tu nguoi dung.
- Danh sach duoc sap xep theo thu tu gui truoc xu ly truoc.
- Xem email, ma tai khoan, ho ten va thoi gian gui yeu cau.
- Tra cuu tai khoan bang email, MSSV hoac MSGV.
- Cap mat khau tam thoi cho nguoi dung.
- Gui email khoi phuc mat khau cho nguoi dung.
- Tu dong chuyen yeu cau sang trang thai da xu ly sau khi gui email thanh cong.

Cach nguoi dung gui yeu cau quen mat khau:
1. Tai trang dang nhap, bam "Quen mat khau?" hoac truy cap /forgot.
2. Nhap email, MSSV hoac MSGV.
3. Bam "Gui yeu cau khoi phuc".
4. He thong luu yeu cau vao bang support voi trang thai pending.

Cach admin xu ly yeu cau:
1. Dang nhap bang tai khoan admin.
2. Vao /nckh-admin.
3. Chon menu "Support".
4. Xem bang "Support request queue".
5. Xu ly yeu cau theo thu tu tu tren xuong duoi, vi yeu cau gui truoc se nam truoc.
6. Bam "Process" tren yeu cau can xu ly.
7. He thong tao mat khau tam thoi, cap nhat mat khau cua tai khoan va gui email cho nguoi dung.
8. Sau khi xu ly xong, yeu cau duoc chuyen sang trang thai processed va khong con nam trong danh sach pending.

Ghi chu:
- Neu nguoi dung nhap ma tai khoan nhu 23211TT2984, he thong se tim email dang luu theo ma tai khoan do.
- Neu nguoi dung nhap email, he thong se tim tai khoan theo email do.
- Sau khi nhan email, nguoi dung dang nhap bang mat khau tam thoi, sau do vao trang ho so/du an de doi lai mat khau moi.
- Email duoc gui theo cau hinh mail hien co cua Laravel.


5. CAC TRANG/DUONG DAN CHINH

- /login: trang dang nhap.
- /forgot: trang gui yeu cau khoi phuc mat khau.
- /nckh-visitor: trang danh cho khach tham quan.
- /visitor-detail: trang chi tiet san pham cho visitor.
- /nckh-student: trang chu sinh vien.
- /upload: trang dang san pham moi.
- /edit-product: trang chinh sua san pham.
- /detail: trang chi tiet san pham cho sinh vien.
- /nckh-teacher: trang chu giang vien.
- /detail-teacher: trang chi tiet san pham cho giang vien.
- /nckh-compare: trang so sanh san pham bang AI cho giang vien.
- /nckh-admin: trang quan tri he thong.
- /nckh-profile: trang ho so ca nhan.


6. GHI CHU VE QUYEN TRUY CAP

- Visitor khong can dang nhap, chi xem va tuong tac voi san pham cong khai.
- Student phai dang nhap moi duoc dang, sua, xoa va xem san pham cua minh.
- Teacher phai dang nhap moi duoc xem danh sach can duyet va duyet/tu choi san pham.
- Admin phai dang nhap moi duoc quan ly nguoi dung, san pham, chuyen nganh va thong ke he thong.
- Admin phai dang nhap moi duoc xem va xu ly yeu cau Support.
- Neu truy cap sai vai tro, he thong se chan quyen hoac dua ve trang khong tim thay/khong duoc phep.


7. QUY TRINH HOAT DONG TONG QUAT

1. Admin tao tai khoan cho student, teacher va admin khac neu can.
2. Student dang nhap va dang san pham/do an.
3. San pham moi duoc dua vao trang thai cho duyet.
4. Teacher xem chi tiet san pham va duyet hoac tu choi.
5. San pham da duyet duoc hien thi cho Visitor xem tren trang trung bay.
6. Visitor co the tim kiem, xem chi tiet, tang luot xem va thich san pham.
7. Neu nguoi dung quen mat khau, nguoi dung gui yeu cau tai /forgot.
8. Admin vao Support de xu ly yeu cau quen mat khau theo thu tu gui truoc xu ly truoc.
9. Admin theo doi thong ke, quan ly du lieu va xu ly khi can.
